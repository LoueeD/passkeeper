# Business Model

Start free and open-source.

Then add paid options after library traction.

## Paid Options

```txt
Hosted dashboard
Managed invite links
Audit logs
User/device management
Team/org management
Email notifications
Compliance exports
Priority support
```

## Possible Pricing

```txt
Open source: free
Hosted Starter: GBP 9-19/month
Hosted Team: GBP 49/month
Pro support: GBP 199/month
```

Do not build billing in the first two weeks.
